Enjoy the Location that make you Relad

Tribhuvan Park

Located in Kathmandu, this peaceful and well-maintained green space is ideal for picnics and gatherings. Visitors praise its “sprawling grounds” and remind one another to help keep it clean by using dustbins.

Taudaha Lake

A serene lakeside escape on the edge of Kathmandu, historically a royal duck farm. It’s popular for birdwatching, boating, and low-impact relaxation.
